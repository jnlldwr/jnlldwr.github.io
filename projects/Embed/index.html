The Most Beautiful Moment in Life, Pt. 1

3rd Mini Album

Released: April 29, 2015

Recorded: 2015

Genre: Hip hop 路 R&B 路 Dance-pop 路 EDM 路 Pop

BTS has come back with their third mini album, The Most Beautiful Moment in Life Pt.1.

No longer just young boys, the album is an introduction to BTS as a group that continues to grow and develop. In each track are distilled poetic and pop emotions that allow the listener to sense both the soft and resounding musical transformation of the group.

The most beautiful moment in life pt.1 focuses on the uncertain future more than the glamour of youth.
