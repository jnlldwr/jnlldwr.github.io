THE MOST BEAUTIFUL MOMENT IN LIFE, Pt. 1

3rd Mini Album

Released: April 29, 2015

Recorded: 2015

Genre: Hip hop · R&B · Dance-pop · EDM · Pop

BTS has come back with their third mini album, The Most Beautiful Moment in Life Pt.1.

No longer just young boys, the album is an introduction to BTS as a group that continues to grow and develop. In each track are distilled poetic and pop emotions that allow the listener to sense both the soft and resounding musical transformation of the group.

The most beautiful moment in life pt.1 focuses on the uncertain future more than the glamour of youth.

THE MOST BEAUTIFUL MOMENT IN LIFE, Pt. 2

3rd Mini Album

Released: November 30, 2015

Recorded: 2015

Genre: Hip hop · R&B · Dance-pop · EDM · Pop

Released seven months after that first installment, The most beautiful moment in life pt.2 sings about the energy of youth barreling forward, embracing even the uncertainty and insecurity.

Now beginning to taste the bitterness of grown-up life, they can easily sense that life isn't always beautiful. However, they continue to sprint forward, relying only on their two destitute legs and the fire in their hearts. There's nothing else to do but keep running. Facing the powerlessness of one's youth, they collide and shatter and still cry out that “it's okay to fall, it's okay to get hurt.” That is the beauty of this moment.

THE MOST BEAUTIFUL MOMENT IN LIFE, Young Forever

Compilation Album

Released: May 2, 2016

Recorded: 2015-2016

Genre: Hip hop · R&B · Dance-pop · EDM · Pop

The final chapter of the story of The most beautiful moment in life.

Having won over the world with their two-part The most beautiful moment in life series in 2015, BTS will release their special album The most beautiful moment in life: YOUNG FOREVER on May 2. True to the title of the albums, the series has enabled BTS to now be in the most beautiful moment of their lives. The most beautiful moment in life: YOUNG FOREVER is a special album that marks the conclusion of the epic journey of the series, containing the last stories told by young people who, despite an uncertain and insecure reality (The most beautiful moment in life pt.1) continue to surge forward (The most beautiful moment in life pt.2).
