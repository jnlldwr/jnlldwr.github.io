about me

Hi! I'm Janelle Faith Diawara, an Information Technology student at the Polytechnic University of the Philippines. I have a deep passion for technology and problem-solving, with a particular interest in web development and e-commerce. Over the years, I've gained hands-on experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Java, and have worked on various web development projects ranging from dynamic websites to integrated payment systems.

Database management has been a critical skill, especially during my time at the Collecting and Disbursing Office, where I ensured the accuracy and integrity of financial records. In addition to my technical tasks, I provided support to students with inquiries about the collecting and disbursing processes. I found great satisfaction in helping them navigate these administrative waters, ensuring their transactions were smooth and accurate. A part of the job that I liked was assisting in the preparation and organization of crucial documents like liquidation reports and certifications.
